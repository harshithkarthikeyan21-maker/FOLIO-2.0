 Folio: AI-Driven Financial Portfolio Advisor
Project Tagline
FOLIO: The unified, AI-driven portfolio advisor that connects your fragmented investments and transforms complex market data into instant, plain-English risk analysis.

Project Overview
Folio is an innovative, real-time financial portfolio management and advisory platform designed to simplify how everyday investors interact with the stock market. Instead of forcing users to navigate multiple scattered tracking tools and complex, intimidating financial spreadsheets, Folio provides a single, centralized workspace that makes managing wealth intuitive and clear.

The Problem It Solves
Fragmented Investments: Most investors hold assets across multiple different brokerage platforms, making it incredibly slow and difficult to see their complete financial picture at once.

Lack of Intelligent Risk Analysis: Traditional platforms show simple balance totals but fail to explain hidden vulnerabilities, causing everyday investors to overlook heavy risk concentrations or miss vital market opportunities.

Complex Data Overload: Understanding asset diversification, market trends, and portfolio health typically requires hours of dense study or relying on generic analytics that aren't tailored to an individual's actual profile.

Core Features For Future
Unified Asset Dashboard: Automatically aggregates and structures your entire investment history across various brokers into one clean, comprehensive view.

Automated Activity Tracking: A streamlined interface to instantly record your trade history, tracking transactions effortlessly without manual asset organization headaches.

AI Advisor Engine: Dynamically analyzes your overall portfolio composition to score its general health, catch overconcentration, and deliver actionable diversification strategies.

Interactive AI Chat: Features a conversational interface that lets you talk directly to your data, translating complicated financial metrics into straightforward, plain-English insights.
